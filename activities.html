Exciting Activities Await You

Zumba

Join us for an invigorating Zumba session! Experience the joy of dance combined with fitness, as our talented instructor guides you through energetic and rhythmic movements. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or a beginner, Zumba is a fun way to stay active and healthy.

Cook-off

Showcase your culinary prowess in our thrilling Cook-off competition! Unleash your creativity in the kitchen and prepare a dish that wows the judges. Whether you're a master chef or a cooking enthusiast, this is your chance to impress with your gastronomic skills.

Dance Competition

Get ready to hit the dance floor in our exciting Dance Competition! Showcase your unique style, creativity, and choreography. Whether you're into hip-hop, salsa, or contemporary dance, this competition welcomes all genres. Join us for a night of rhythm and expression!

Singing Contest

Let your voice be heard in our enchanting Singing Contest! Whether you're a seasoned vocalist or someone who enjoys singing in the shower, this contest is your opportunity to shine. Choose your favorite song, captivate the audience, and compete for the title of the event's top singer.

Spoken Poetry

Express yourself through the power of words in our Spoken Poetry session. Share your thoughts, emotions, and stories through eloquent and heartfelt poetry. Whether you're a seasoned poet or trying spoken word for the first time, this is a platform to inspire and be inspired.
